## Tour 2026 - Art of Loving

2026 Tour Timetable

To an eagerly awaiting audience, multi-BRIT Award, GRAMMY and Mercury Prize nominee Olivia Dean unveils her long-awaited sophomore album; The Art Of Loving, produced with Zach Nahome; a record glimmering in the thoughtful exploration of love, in its many forms.

Effortlessly blending warmth and honesty throughout her heartfelt depictions of romantic and platonic love, Olivia elegantly displays her honed and mastered musical craft through her ever-resonant songwriting.
